MISCONCEPTION #1

"Your org is only for tech nerds, I won't fit in."

We emphasize how UX solves problems in various disciplines, not just in tech. We have tech pros, yes, but we’re a diverse community where anyone’s sure to fit in and everyone can learn from each other!

MISCONCEPTION #2

"You're like a serious org. You never have fun!"

We do, in fact, have loads of fun! We offer many events that not only put the cool in school, but also bring the earn in learn. This year, especially, we have a lot of events that aims to help create a bond with the members while teaching them the essentials of UX design. See you there ;)

MISCONCEPTION #3

"UX Society? I've never heard of that org (lol)."

Well now you have and we’re glad you did! While we’re a relatively new org, we have grown credibility in and out of Ateneo for the many projects we have contributed. We promise you’ll learn a lot that’ll help you in and out of college if you give it a try!
